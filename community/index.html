    Community Resources¶

    Welcome! This page highlights resources built and maintained by the Agent Development Kit community.

    Info

    Google and the ADK team do not provide support for the content linked in these external community resources.

    Join the Community¶

    • Want to discuss ADK, ask questions, or talk about all things agents? Head to r/agentdevelopmentkit on Reddit.
    • Want updates on the monthly community call? Join the ADK Community Google Group.
    • Want to file a bug or contribute to the ADK framework? Check out the Contributing Guide to find the right repo and get started.
